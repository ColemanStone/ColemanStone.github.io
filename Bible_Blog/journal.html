My entire life I have had one thing always be there and that one thing is music. This verse is always in the back of my mind whenever I am playing music either on stage or alone. Ever since I started learning piano when I was about 4 or 5, I haven't stopped. I learned guitar and drums along the way to today. I am able to use that gift God game me to worship him in a way where I feel connected to him whenever I play on Sundays. 11/10/25

I have never had an "experience" with God or the Holy Spirit in the sense of hearing or seeing God clear as day. But I do have something that I do experience. Whenever I have something weigh on my mind, I have vivid dreams about that situation. It could be me playing a new song or even asking someone to go and do something. No matter what it is, it either goes good or very, very bad. There has never been a thing that went bad in my dream actually go bad when it happens in my life. But the things I worry about going wrong appear in my dreams tend to go good. It also goes good in real life after that. "may the God of hope fill you with joy and peace as you trust in him, so that you may overflow with hope by the power of the Holy Spirit." Romans 15:13. This verse makes me think those vivid dreams I have is the Holy Spirit working in me to make me more confident and not question myself.
