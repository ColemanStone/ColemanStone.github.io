The 2025 Morgan Reunion will be the entire week of June 16th to June 21st 2025. The Morgan dinner will be saturday June 21st. Before the dinner all week there will be something to do every day.

Monday June 16th: Boating on the lake. We will be renting boats and having a good time on the lake.

Wednesday June 18th: Golfing at the Wildcat Golf Course. We will be having a golf outing.

Wednesday June 18th: Flower Picking at the Morgan Flower Farm. We will be picking flowers and making bouquets.

Saturday June 19th: Morgan Family Dinner. We will be having a dinner at the Morgan Family House.
