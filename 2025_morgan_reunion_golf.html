2025 Morgan Reunion Golf Outing

Join us for the 2025 Morgan Reunion Golf Outing at the Wildcat Golf Course. The outing will be held on Saturday, June 14th, 2025. The outing will begin at 8:00 am and will include 18 holes of golf, a cart, and lunch. The cost is $50 per person. This will be a scramble with all the teams. So a team consisting of up to 4 golfers will play best ball every hole. Please RSVP by June 1st, 2025. We hope to see you there! This will be a unoffical tournment. The team that has the best score wins the Wildcat Morgan Cup.
